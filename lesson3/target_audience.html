Persona "A"

Event planner

Outdoor event specialist, a Male or Female Adult (18 to 65 years old) who needs to be sure about temperature and raining for making crucial decisions for event details.

Scenarios

Outdoor weeding

Event specialist interested for the weather, to be sure the temperature to make decisions about decoration details, type of drinks or foods, wedding dress, and so on. Also they need to avoid raining situations setting the day.

Outdoor music concert

Before expensive advertising campaign on international broadcast tv, radio, newspaper; before selling thousands of music event tickets; before making all the installation for the electronic equipment; the event planner need to know the weather schedule to save millions of dollars.

Persona "B"

Adventure seekers

A Male or Female, Young or Adult (15 to 35 years old) who likes to hike, go fishing, camps, much more. Is worried about safety precautions to avoid accidents.

Scenarios

Hiking adventure Company

Could be obvious that a storm will ruin your hiking weekend, contracts for days like this shouldn’t been available, and message should be sent to clients each time the schedule must be change for unexpected and dangerous climate changes.
