White Space

Telegram
https://desktop.telegram.org/

There are enough space between download button (Get Telegram for Windows) and the second option (Show all platforms) to clearly separate Telegram for Windows from the other Telegram’s platforms. Also, the next big white space, state a bigger difference for the next type of content, because isn’t related anymore with any kind of Telegram platform. The next information is made for developer who will contribute.

Fitt’s Law

TeamViewer
Link to the website (just click)

If you are holding your mobile using one hand the big finger will be really close to the bottom of the screen. The closer is the button from the finger, the sooner will be the conversion (the click), enhancing the probabilities for the app to be downloaded successfully.
